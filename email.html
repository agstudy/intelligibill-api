How do we calculate your saving?
We get this calculation by comparing your bill against every public deal in our database. It's updated daily. The deal we quote assumes your latest bill is typical for you. If it's not, we can recalculate your deal. The deal is available for both residential and business customers. Because we're independent we don't take commissions from retailers. That means we compare your deal with plans from every retailer.
